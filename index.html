AQUARIUM EVENTS?

Aquariums host a variety of events, including educational programs, interactive exhibits, behind-the-scenes tours, feeding demonstrations, and special themed events like underwater shows and marine conservation awareness days. Check the aquarium's schedule for specific details on upcoming events.

TICKET'S PRICE?

The ticket prices for aquarium events can vary depending on the specific details of the event and the location of the aquarium. For instance, regular admission for adults might range from $20 to $40, with discounted rates for children, seniors, and students. Special events or themed exhibits may have separate pricing.
